## Section: Common Sub-Zero Freezer Failures

### H2
`What Goes Wrong With Sub-Zero Freezers`

### Lead
`Sub Zero freezers fail in patterns that rarely require sealed-system work. Defrost components, evaporator fans, and door seals account for the vast majority of calls. Here's the spread.`

### Failure cards (8)

**Sub-Zero freezer not freezing**
Fridge cold, freezer warm. Most likely the evaporator fan motor failed or the defrost system iced over the coil. Usually under 60 minutes diagnostic + repair.

**Sub-Zero ice buildup on freezer walls**
Defrost heater, defrost timer, or defrost thermostat failure. Common on units 8+ years old. Defrost system parts are inexpensive — labor is the cost.

**Sub-Zero freezer column warming up**
Column freezer specifically — almost always the evaporator fan. Symptoms: ice cream softens, food doesn't fully freeze, compressor still cycles normally.

**Sub-Zero freezer making clicking or buzzing**
Compressor relay, condenser fan bearing, or evaporator fan blade hitting ice buildup. Each sound has a distinctive signature.

**Sub-Zero freezer running constantly**
Door gasket failure (most common), defrost termination thermostat stuck open, or condenser blocked with dust. Door gasket repairs are the highest-ROI service we do.

**Sub-Zero freezer drawer not closing**
Drawer slide, gasket compression, or food obstruction. Common on Built-In freezer drawers and Pro 48 freezer columns.

**Sub-Zero freezer error code (E1, E2, EE, etc.)**
Modern Sub-Zero freezers self-diagnose. We arrive with the service bulletin pulled for your specific error.

**Sub-Zero freezer leaking water**
Defrost drain blockage. Water from defrost cycle has nowhere to go and pools at the bottom of the cabinet. Usually a 30-minute clear.

## Section: FAQ (8 — gets FAQPage schema)

**1. How fast can you repair my Sub-Zero freezer in South Florida?**
Same-day in most of Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach if you call before noon. Marine and Monroe County calls may need a half-day buffer.

**2. My freezer is warm but my refrigerator is cold. What's wrong?**
On a column freezer, almost certainly the freezer evaporator fan. On a side-by-side, could be the fan or the air damper between sections. Diagnostic is fast.

**3. There's ice buildup on the back wall of my freezer. Is that normal?**
No. It means the defrost system has failed. Defrost heater, defrost timer, or defrost thermostat. Inexpensive parts, fixable in one visit.

**4. My Sub-Zero freezer makes a loud clicking sound. What is it?**
Compressor relay attempting to start, or condenser fan bearing on its way out. Distinct sounds — diagnostic in minutes.

**5. Should I replace my freezer or repair it?**
For defrost or fan failures: always repair. For full compressor replacement on a 25+ year unit: it's a conversation. We give honest answers.

**6. Do you fix legacy Sub-Zero freezers like the 524 or 642?**
Yes. We have parts for legacy units back to the 1990s.

**7. My freezer is leaking water onto the floor. What now?**
Defrost drain is blocked. Water from the defrost cycle has nowhere to go. Usually a 30-minute service call, sometimes less.

**8. Are repairs warrantied?**
Yes. Workmanship warranty in writing on the invoice. OEM parts carry manufacturer warranty on top.
