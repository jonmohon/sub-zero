## Common Sub-Zero Failures in Boca Raton

**1. Defrost system failures on 2008-2014 BI series installs**
The major gated-community kitchen renovations of the late 2000s installed thousands of BI-36 and BI-42 units across Boca. Defrost timer and defrost thermostat failures around the 12-year mark are now the dominant call.

**2. Wine column humidity drift**
Boca Raton is wine country — dual-zone wine columns are everywhere. Humidity targeting drifts after 8-10 years and most owners never know until corks start drying. Recalibration is preventive maintenance worth scheduling annually.

**3. Snowbird-cycle return damage**
Half-occupancy homes face seasonal voltage cycling and humidity swings that stress Sub-Zero electronics. Common pre-season service: control board check, evaporator coil clean, gasket inspection.

**4. Pro 48 sealed system gradual cooling loss**
Single-family Pro 48 installs from the 2010s renovation wave hit their first major sealed-system service window now. Pressure test, locate, repair, recharge — usually one visit.

**5. Ice maker water filter neglect**
Boca's harder water profile pushes water filter replacement to the lower end of the manufacturer's recommended interval. Skipping replacement leads to clogged inlet valves and small/cloudy ice cubes.

**3. I'm a snowbird and my Sub-Zero sat unused for 6 months. What should I check?**
Three things on every snowbird return: (1) door gaskets (compression loss is common), (2) ice maker fill tube (can develop blockages), and (3) condenser coils (dust accumulation during dormancy). We offer a flat-rate "snowbird return service" that covers all three plus a system check — worth scheduling within the first month back.

**4. Are wine column humidity recalibrations worth doing if I'm not having problems?**
Yes — humidity drift is invisible until corks start failing, by which time damage is done. Annual recalibration is preventive maintenance in the truest sense. We include recalibration as part of our wine column service automatically, even when the call is for a different issue.

**5. Do you service Wolf and Cove appliances in Boca too?**
Yes. Sub-Zero, Wolf (ranges, ovens, cooktops), and Cove (dishwashers) are all part of the Sub-Zero Group product family and our team services the full lineup. Boca kitchens routinely have all three brands together — we can handle multi-appliance service in a single visit.
