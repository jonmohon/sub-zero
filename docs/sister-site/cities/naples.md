## Common Sub-Zero Failures in Naples

**1. Pre-season return diagnostics on snowbird homes**
Half-occupancy Naples homes face significant seasonal stress on Sub-Zero units — months of dormancy, voltage cycles, humidity swings. Pre-season service catches problems before owners return.

**2. Pro 48 sealed-system service on Pelican Bay / Grey Oaks installs**
The big wave of Pelican Bay and Grey Oaks 2000s renovations installed dozens of Pro 48 units. They're at 15-20 years now and sealed system service is the expected next call.

**3. Wine column humidity drift on luxury home installs**
Naples wine collectors are serious — most luxury homes have multi-zone wine columns or dedicated wine rooms. Humidity drift after 8-10 years is the hidden enemy. Recalibration restores correct targeting.

**4. Marine refrigerator vibration leaks on yachts**
Naples Boat Club and Naples Yacht Club have a substantial Sub-Zero marine fleet. Vibration-induced refrigerant loss is the dominant marine failure pattern.

**5. Old Naples condenser dust from heavy landscaping**
Old Naples is heavily landscaped with mature canopy and active yard maintenance. Condenser dust accumulation is faster here than in newer developments. Annual cleaning prevents premature compressor wear.
