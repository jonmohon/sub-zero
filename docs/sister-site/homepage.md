## Section: FAQ (8 entries — gets FAQPage schema)

**1. How fast can you get to me for Sub-Zero repair in South Florida?**
Most appointments inside Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach counties get a same-day window. Marine appointments and trips into Monroe County may require a half-day buffer depending on slip access. Booking before noon dramatically improves same-day odds.

**2. Do you charge extra for weekend or holiday service?**
No. The flat rate is the flat rate. Tuesday afternoon, Saturday night, Christmas Day — same number. There is no after-hours line item on our invoices.

**3. Are you Sub-Zero Factory Certified?**
Our technicians are factory-trained on Sub-Zero, Wolf, and Cove product lines and we use only OEM parts. Whether your unit qualifies for any specific manufacturer warranty work depends on the model and warranty status — we will confirm that for you when you book.

**4. What models of Sub-Zero do you service?**
Every active product line: Built-In (BI series), Integrated (IT/IC), Designer, Column, Pro 48, Classic, and Undercounter. We also work on legacy lines back to the original 532, 561, 632, and 690 series.

**5. Do you cover marine refrigeration?**
Yes. Dockside service across South Florida marinas — Miami Beach Marina, Sunset Harbour, Bal Harbour, Crandon Park, Pier 66, Bahia Mar, and the major Naples and Marco Island basins.

**6. What's your warranty?**
Workmanship warranty on the labor for every repair, plus the OEM manufacturer warranty on any genuine part we install. Both appear on your invoice in writing.

**7. Do you service Wolf or Cove appliances too?**
Yes. Sub-Zero, Wolf (ranges, ovens, cooktops), and Cove (dishwashers) are part of the same Sub-Zero Group product family and our team services the full lineup.

**8. How do I get a quote without a service visit?**
For most diagnostics, we need eyes on the unit — but if you describe the symptom and provide the model number when you call, we can usually narrow the likely range before the technician arrives.
